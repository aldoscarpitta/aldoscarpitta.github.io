Harbours
<h1>Harbours</h1> <p>2022 - ongoing</p> <br> <p>A soundwalk transforms listening into a viscous encounter, where sound's honey-like materiality grasps us as we grasp it, creating a reciprocal embrace. Each step moves through sonic volumes—not measured in decibels but as invisible expanses that hold walkers and environments together. The soundscape becomes an indivisible sphere where we exist not as separate listeners but as interconnected beings co-creating meaning through the ephemeral spaces between things. This mobile dimensionality reveals the world's capacity rather than its boundaries, creating both an acoustic geography of the unseen and an embodied memory of our temporal co-existence within sound's formless embrace.</p> <br> <p>"Genoa Walk" selected by New Sound Fields 2024</p>
