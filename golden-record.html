<img src="golden-record.jpg" alt="Golden Record"> <h1>Golden Record</h1> <p>2023</p> <p>Biennale College Teatro 2023 - Finalist Project</p> <br> <p>Golden Record is a site-specific immersive project exploring space, sound, and body through collective creation. The project asks: what remains when all is gone? Moments survive as echoes at the edge of memory, leaving traces of existence that bridge life and death.</p> <br> <p>The project establishes a temporary platform for ecoacoustic research, starting with a workshop and culminating in a public installation. Participants wear wireless headphones in a darkened space, sharing a common soundscape while moving freely among microphones that act as sonic time capsules. The experience plays with the contrast between visual space and acoustic intimacy, where every sound becomes a significant event. The sonic identity of a place holds its memories and stories, giving the soundscape archaeological value. This creates an environment where the line between performer and audience blurs, and outcomes emerge unpredictably through collective interaction with the space.</p> <br> <p>Curator: Aldo Scarpitta</p> <p>With: Francesco Cecchi Aglietti, Serena De Siena, Lisa Lendaro, Manfredi Messana, Ivo Randaccio</p> <p>Supported by: Biennale di Venezia</p>
