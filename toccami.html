Toccami
<h1>Toccami</h1> <p>2022</p> <p>Casa Maqroll</p> <br> <p>Aldo Scarpitta, Eleonora Longobardi, with the support of Progetto Cantiere, Festival Incanti, La Tartana Teatro, Movin'Up</p> <br> <p>Toccami (Touch Me) is a multidisciplinary show with a circular plan and an interactive device, involving different performing arts (music, puppet theatre, narration) in order to create an immersive and playful experience aimed at exploring the theme of sexual pleasure in relation to self-discovery.</p>
