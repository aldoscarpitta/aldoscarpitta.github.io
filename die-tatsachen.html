Die Tatsachen auf dem Tisch
<h1>Die Tatsachen auf dem Tisch</h1> <p>2022, Dresden/Palermo/Catania</p> <br> <p>Aldo Scarpitta, Barbara Lubich and Daniela Lehman, with the support of the Ministry of Culture</p> <br> <p>The performative installation "Die Tatsachen liegen auf dem Tisch" addresses the relationship between community and memory starting from a position of attentive listening. We are inspired by old and new sound recordings, historical photographs and the site itself. An experience which explores memory as body, sound and matter and experiments methods to access the past beyond the historical information.</p> <br> <p>Zentralwerk in Dresden (Germany) is an Independent cultural centre built between 1939 and 1942 as an armament factory.</p> <br>
