<p>Aldo is an improviser, sound-artist, and curator who creates site-specific experiences to enable collective listening. His work investigates how time enables encounters and connections between people across different moments, using sound to reveal the relationships between communities, spaces, and their histories. Working with participants as co-creators, his practice has evolved from individual consciousness exploration toward collaborative investigation, where listening becomes a method for discovering how strangers can connect through shared acoustic experiences.</p>
